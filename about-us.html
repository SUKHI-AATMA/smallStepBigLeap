Our Philosophy

Our indulgences often lead to a consistent neglect of our most prized assets - body and mind.What we eat has a direct bearing on how we feel and think.

At SSBL, we believe in a diligent adaption and practice of two age old adages - ‘Health is Wealth’ and ‘Prevention is better than Cure’. Together, they perfectly complement to articulate our thinking.

Amidst the hectic lifestyles we lead, most of us are caught between either of the following conundrums.

  • Eating has become a tick mark activity, just necessary to satiate hunger rather than fulfil the nutritional needs that our body craves.
  • ‘Live to eat’ rather than ‘eat to live’- By becoming slaves to our taste buds, we are unable to ‘eat right’ even when aware that we are ‘eating wrong’
  • Crash diets, short term plans help address weight related challenges only temporarily. They reappear after some time
  • Relying on self-study, guidance from friends and family to plan family meals which we believe/think are nutritious and balanced

At SSBL, we encourage respecting food and responsibly consuming it for its manifold virtues. For it is not just food but:

  • A powerful tool to nourish our bodies and mind, making them stronger and sharper
  • A potent weapon to build invaluable immunity to fight infections
  • A holistic solution to hasten recovery from any health challenges being faced
  • An effective catalyst for leading a wholesome, happy, confident and healthy life

We collaborate with you to bridge the gap between where you are and where you want to be in your pursuit of better health and happiness, via food. Our expertise can be tapped via awareness building sessions, workshops, corporate programmes as well as customised diet and nutrition plans for individuals and families.

Our Mission

“Celebrate Life”

Celebration is a manifestation of happiness and wellbeing is its core. This feeling of wellbeing is what you sense when your body and mind are in good health as well as harmony. The resulting strength of emotions and positivity make you feel on ‘top of the world’.

Meenakshi Bhalla

My own experience and learnings have inspired me to start SSBL.

An MBA by qualification, I spent the first two decades of my career in the ever demanding corporate sector within the IT industry at blue chip names – Wipro, Tata Communication, Gartner, and MindTree. I have experienced and closely witnessed the adoption of quick fix measures – pop a pill when feeling sick, grab a smoke or drink to ease stress, skip a meal or overeat as time permits, eat out or order takeaway often, binge on processed food and finally the havoc this creates in our body over time.

Chronic deficiency of macro and micro nutrients in my body resulted in severe health issues and made the basic need of a good night’s sleep an unattainable luxury. Visits to several doctors and specialists, a battery of diagnostic tests and several rounds of medication followed. But at best they provided intermittent, short term relief while making my body weaker.

I was physically and emotionally in turmoil when I finally found my saviour in nutrition as therapy that I got introduced to by an eminent Dietician. A customised, calibrated diet, did wonders to heal my body and mind. My ailments disappeared with time and I also received the gifts of positivity and happiness. My holistic, sustainable healing empowered me to Celebrate Life once again!

This miracle that I experienced inspired me to unravel and share the ‘Power of Nutrition’. Quitting the corporate sector, I indulged headlong in my new passion by acquiring requisite qualifications in this field i.e. M.Sc. in Dietetics and a teacher’s training certification in Yoga.

Working with a leading hospital chain, I engaged with patients suffering from myriad ailments and observed the difference that a nutritive diet made in their recovery. Better the nutrition, faster the recovery and in some cases just the right diet was the medicine.

The mantra to stay happy is very simple - just take a Small Step to nourish your body with nutritious food, enjoy a regular physical activity of choice and keep your mind constructively engaged to for a richer, happier life.

By starting Small Step Big Leap, I am taking a small step to share my experience and learning towards creating awareness about the significance of focusing on ‘self’. I am joined in this endeavour by a team eminent experts. We share the common belief that happiness and wellbeing are two sides of the same coin.

We aspire to help people rediscover happiness and Celebrate Life! Our ‘small step’, we hope, will result in a ‘BIG LEAP’ towards creating a happier society and a healthier nation.
