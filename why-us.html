Our Approach

We tend to take a casual approach to our health by often ignoring symptoms that may be early alerts to something more serious. Only when our functionality is actually hampered do we consult a doctor seeking quick remedy.

Change in appetite, dryness in throat, sudden shift in weight, increased thirst, constipation or multiple stools, urge to pass urine frequently can be early indications of an underlying or developing illness, which can perhaps be prevented by taking a timely small step to lead a little more disciplined life.
